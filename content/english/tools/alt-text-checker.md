Missing or incorrect alt texts are the most common accessibility issue on websites. In our audits, **WCAG success criterion 1.1.1** (Non-text Content) fails most often — on nearly every website we review.

With this alt text checker you enter a URL and instantly see which images are on the page, what information screen readers receive, and where alt texts are missing.
