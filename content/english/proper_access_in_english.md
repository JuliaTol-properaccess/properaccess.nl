# Accessibility Audits

We provide in-depth accessibility audits for websites and mobile applications, fully aligned with the **European standard EN 301 549**. This standard underpins digital accessibility legislation in the EU, including the Web Accessibility Directive and the European Accessibility Act (EAA).

Share your **URL** with us, and we'll provide you with a project estimate—**no obligations**.

Our audit reports are designed to meet the requirements of public sector bodies and private organizations that must prepare official accessibility statements in accordance with national regulations. We ensure that your organization has a clear, actionable overview of accessibility barriers and a roadmap to compliance.

Our audit process includes:

- **Manual Testing** based on the latest WCAG 2.1 - WCAG 2.2. AA guidelines and EN 301 549 requirements.
    
- **Assistive Technology Testing** to assess real-world usability for people using screen readers, keyboard navigation, and other tools.
    
- **Clear, Actionable Reports** that meet the standards expected by **European public authorities** and are suitable as input for accessibility declarations.
    

We serve **government organizations and companies** across Europe that are required to comply with the **EAA**. Our current clients include organizations in **the Netherlands, Sweden, and Germany**.

    Our team consists of **12 full-time, in-house trained auditors**, all dedicated exclusively to accessibility. We are **WAS-certified by the International Association of Accessibility Professionals (IAAP)**, ensuring deep technical and standards-based knowledge.

    Every audit report we deliver is **reviewed by at least three accessibility experts** to guarantee accuracy and consistency. This multi-layered quality assurance ensures reliable results you can act on with confidence.
