Not another report gathering dust

Most audit firms deliver a report per success criterion. Fifty pages of flat text, technically correct, but impossible for your team to work with. We do things differently.

Our report is organised per web page and per element. Your developer opens the report, sees exactly which element on which page needs to be fixed, and gets to work. With screenshots, code examples, and concrete solutions.

The result? Faster turnaround, fewer misunderstandings, and a website that actually becomes more accessible.

Our approach

Thorough research

We test your website with screen readers, keyboard, magnification software, and voice control. Not just automated — manually, the way your visitors experience it.

Concrete solutions

For every issue, you get a concrete solution or a recommended approach. No “make this accessible”, but actionable advice your team can pick up right away.

Direct access to senior auditor

Direct access to a senior auditor for any questions about the findings. No account managers or project managers in between. Questions? Just call.
